About Us

HAWK is a Non Profit Company aiming to get local township people involved in providing better care for their animals and themselves.

Founded by Andre Verbiest, we work closely with V-Paws Animal Rescue Organisation to rescue and rehabilitate animals as well as to educate poor communities about animal care in the greater Cape Town area.

HAWK operates from a plot in Chatsworth, Malmesbury where we care for over 300 dogs and cats living on the premises.

Andre with the help of six full-time workers feed and care for the animals 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.



How We Started

Andre Verbiest discovered a pregnant dog, tied to a railway line with wire.

As a result of this inhumane cruelty. Lucy (as she quickly became known) lost her front leg due to several trains passing over her.

The name Lucky was added because the trauma did not cause her death.

Today Lucky Lucy lives with Andre amongst 300 other animals and lives life to the fullest - proving that not only do we need animals in our lives, they need a human who will love and protect them no matter what - which is what Andre and the team at HAWK do.



How We Help

Animal Rescue - We provide a safe haven for animals in need.

Veterinary Services - We provide veterniary services to those animals in need and help the community by teaching them to care for their animals.

Fostering and Adoption - We help find their temporary and forever homes.

Feeding the community - We help provide food parcels for the community children.

Teaching the community - We help to educate the community about better care for their animals.



How You Can Help

We are in desperate need of funds for the daily operations of HAWK in order to care for our 300+ animals.

We require food donations and also monthly financial aid for the following expenses :

- rental fo the property and the utilities bill,

- wages of our six full time workers.

- medical expenses of the sick animals and transport costs.

We are currently also raising funds to purchase a vehicle to transport animal food to our premises, transport sick animals to the veterinary clinics and collecting donated building material to build kennels for all our animals.



Helping the Community

HAWK also participates in feeding schemes for the community children.

Our motto is -

'joint participation for, by and in the local communities, for the well being of an animals and humans'.

HAWK is a Non Profit Organization with an initiative to get local township people involved in providing better care for their animals and themselves through participation within the community and our loyal supporters - we are able to do this via various workshops and events.

Where to find us

We are on Riverlands Road, Chatsworth Area about 15kms for Malmesbury.

Put 'Riverlands Primary School' into your GPS.The primary school will be on your left hand side, carry on straight onto a dirt road.

Just after the school you will see a purple house on your left hand side, carry on straight past this house onto the dirt road.

Road is a dirt road, so please drive safely.Eventually you will get to the T junction.Turn right, you will then cross over a little man made bridge (dont worry, its safe).

You will get to a scrap yeard with tyres.Turn left and keep left and the animal shelter will be on your left hand side.
